Hacky Hour

A fun, informal weekly meetup for all computer programmers in Happy Valley, from the amateur to the professional.

Every Thursday from 5:30pm - 8pm
@ New Leaf Initiative

Come out to Hacky Hour. You'll meet other cool developers. You'll learn about new languages, new tools, and new techniques. You can just hang out and code, or you can pair up with someone on a project. Maybe you'll meet a collaborator for your project. Maybe you'll make the connection that leads to your next job. You'll probably learn something new. And at the very least, there will be punch and pie. It's free. Every Thursday evening from 5:30pm-8pm @ New Leaf Initiative.
